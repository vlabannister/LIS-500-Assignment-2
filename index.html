A Little About Victoria Bannister

Victoria Bannister lives in Madison, WI, and is a 4th year part-time student in the Master of Library and Information Studies (MLIS) Distance Learning Program. She currently works full-time at the Department of Public Instruction (DPI) on the Literacy and Mathematics team. Outside of work and school, Victoria enjoys spending time chasing after her toddler!
